The Critical Mandates

Immediate Priorities

100%

Targeted mapping of all viable ward-level boreholes within year one.

Universal Water Access

Water is not a luxury; it is the foundation of health and economic dignity. Machakos has suffered long enough from uncompleted water projects.

  • Push for Senate legislation increasing equitable share allocations for semi-arid counties specifically for water infrastructure.
  • Audit the Thwake Dam reticulation plan to ensure local Machakos residents are prioritized.
  • Sponsor the "County Water Transparency Bill" to track every borehole sunk by the county government.

Youth Empowerment & Innovation

Our greatest natural resource is not under our soil, but in the minds of our youth. We must transition from manual labor dependency to the digital economy.

  • Lobby for national government partnerships to establish Free Wi-Fi Tech Hubs in all 8 sub-counties.
  • Create a legislative framework for the 'Machakos Youth Enterprise Fund' with zero-interest startup loans.
  • Shift focus to TVET modernization, linking local graduates directly with industries in Athi River and Mavoko.

Agriculture & Food Security

Empowering the rural farmer is the quickest way to stimulate the local economy. We must protect our farmers from exploitation and climate volatility.

  • Sponsor legislation protecting mango and citrus farmers from middle-men cartels.
  • Advocate for national subsidies on drought-resistant seeds and specialized fertilizers for Ukambani soil.
  • Push for the establishment of county-level value-addition factories to process surplus produce.

The Senator's Core Mandate

Uncompromising Transparency & Accountability

A Senator does not build roads; a Senator ensures that the money meant for roads is actually spent on roads. I will be the fiercest watchdog for Machakos County's resources.

Quarterly Public Audits

Publishing easy-to-read county expenditure reports for citizens.

Citizen Assemblies

Bi-annual town halls in every sub-county to collect public views.

The First 100 Days in the Senate

01

The Transparency Motion

Table a motion requiring the Machakos County Government to publish a digital, itemized breakdown of all active contractors and project statuses.

02

Bursary Audit Committee

Initiate a Senate inquiry into the equitable distribution of bursary funds to ensure historical disparities across wards are immediately corrected.

National Water Partnership

Convene a summit with the Ministry of Water and local leaders to draft an accelerated timeline for the completion of stalled water projects in the county.
