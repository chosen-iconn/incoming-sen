Commitment to Machakos

A Vision Built for the People

My agenda prioritizes practical, modern solutions tailored for the distinct needs of our sub-counties. Real leadership means tangible results.

Youth Employment

Creating tech hubs and vocational pathways to turn Machakos youth into globally competitive talent.

Modern Education

Upgrading ECDE centers and providing comprehensive bursary programs based on merit and extreme need.

Accessible Healthcare

Equipping Level 3 and 4 hospitals with adequate drugs and motivated personnel to serve our communities.

Agriculture & Water

Subsidizing fertilizers, providing drought-resistant seeds, and drilling strategic boreholes across dry wards.

Infrastructure

Ensuring transparent allocation of road maintenance funds to connect rural farmers to urban markets.

Small Businesses

Establishing the Machakos Enterprise Fund to give low-interest capital to mama mbogas and young entrepreneurs.

The Journey

Rooted in Machakos, Driven by Purpose

Born and raised in the heart of Machakos, my journey began in these very dust roads and bustling markets. I understand the weight of the water jerrican, the anxiety of a delayed harvest, and the unyielding ambition of our youth.

Over the last decade, I have dedicated myself to civic duty, bridging the gap between governance and grassroots realities. I believe a Senator’s role goes beyond oversight in Nairobi; it is about bringing the national cake home and ensuring every shilling works for the wananchi.

"Leadership is not a title; it is the courage to carry the hopes of your community and the integrity to deliver on them."

Real Challenges.
Practical Solutions.

We cannot ignore the realities on the ground. Here is how we transition from political rhetoric to legislative action.

Water Scarcity

The Issue: Extended droughts leaving families without reliable water access.
The Solution: Legislate for higher national allocations towards mega-dams in the region and transparently audit county water budgets.

Youth Unemployment

The Issue: Graduates without opportunities leading to despair.
The Solution: Partner with national and global tech entities to establish digital workspaces in all 8 sub-counties of Machakos.
